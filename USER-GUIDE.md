## Installation
To install the LiveStock App:
1. Go to the official app store or website.
2. Download and install the app on your device.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions for setup. 

## Login Procedure
1. Open the App.
2. Enter your username and password.
3. Click on the "Login" button to access your dashboard.

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your livestock information including:
- Total number of livestock
- Health status
- Feeding logs
- Alerts for health issues

## Adding Livestock
1. Navigate to the "Livestock" section.
2. Click on "Add New Livestock".
3. Fill in the required details:
   - Name
   - Age
   - Breed
   - Health information
4. Save the information to add the livestock to your profile.

## Managing Livestock
In the "Livestock" section, you can:
- Edit livestock details
- Remove livestock from your profile
- View historical data for each animal.

## Health Tracking
The app allows you to:
- Record health events (vaccinations, illnesses)
- Set reminders for veterinary appointments.

## Record Keeping
Keep track of:
- Feeding schedules
- Breeding records
- Sales and purchases of livestock.

## FAQs
- **Q: How can I reset my password?**  
  A: Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.
- **Q: What should I do if I face issues with the app?**  
  A: Contact support through the app or via the website.

## Support
If you have any further questions or need assistance, please reach out to our support team at support@livestockapp.com.
